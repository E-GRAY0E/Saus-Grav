Sausage Gravy

BYOB (bring your own biscuit recipes)

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 lbs breakfast sausage
  • 0.25 cup of butter
  • 0.33 cup of flour
  • 3.25 cups of milk
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 0.125 tsp Italian seasoning

Directions:

  1. Brown sauasge, drain set aside
  2. Melt butter, add flour. cook 1 minute until light brown
  3. Slowly add milk, stir constantly, until thick and bubbly
  4. Add seasonings and sausage
